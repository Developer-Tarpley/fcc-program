Turkey Tortilla Soup

This is a delicious soup dish with a delightful turkey recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 cloves garlic → thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion → chopped
  • 1 chipotle chile in adobo sauce → minced
  • Kosher salt
  • 4 cups homemade turkey stock or low-sodium chicken broth
  • One 15-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups corn tortilla chips → broken into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cup corn kernels → fresh or thawed frozen
  • 2 cups shredded cooked turkey
  • Juice from 2 limes
  • 1/4 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 small avocado → diced → optional

Instructions

    Cooking:

  1. Heat the oil in the saucepan → over medium heat
  2. Add the chili
  3. Add the garlic
  4. Add the onion
  5. Add the chipotle
  6. Add 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Cook stirring until the onion softens → About 5 minutes
  8. Add turkey stock
  9. Add tomatoes
  10. Add 1/2 cup of water
  11. Add half the totilla chips
  12. Bring to a boil
  13. Then reduce heat to a simmer
  14. Cook uncovered until the soup has reduced slightly → About 25 minutes
  15. Add the corn
  16. Finally, add the corn
  17. Cook → 5 minutes

    18. Final steps:

  18. Stir in the turkey and lime juice
  19. Adjust the seasoning with salt → if needed

    20. Servings:

  20. Divide the soup among 4 bowls
  21. Top with the cheese
  22. Top with the cilatro
  23. Top with the remaining tortilla chips
  24. Top with the remaining avocado → if using

Bon Apetit!

And there you go... Enjoy!
